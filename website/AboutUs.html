About Us

Painting.Co is a company that specializes in anything
that has to with painting. We are based in Dirkiesdorp,
Mpumalanga and the company was founded by Themba Khoza
in 2014. We have been in the painting and design industry
for more than 5 years. Our Services include house interior
painting, as well as exterior painting, billboard designing
and painting, logo designs,and more. We are currently only
operating in the province of Mpumalanga but our goal is start
to operate nationwide in a few years to come. The founder of
this company started this company with his two college friends
Chris James and Nokuthula and Madela in 2014. It was always his
dream to be in the Arts and Designs bussiness. Themba's love for
Art comes from his late father, Williams Themba, who used to be
a huge fan of Artwork and Paintings, till the extent he would go
to various kinds of Art galleries atleast twice a month and he would
take Themba with him.That played a huge role in Themba's decision to
pursue a carreer in Art. He studied Art in High School and also in
in University and that really gave him the neccessary knowledge and
skill to start this business.Painting.Co Consists of people with alot
of experience in Designing and Painting and the prices for all these
services we provide are very affordable for any individual
